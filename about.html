Our Journey

From a small startup to industry leader - here's how we've grown and evolved

July 2024

Idea & Foundation

FuelLens was founded with a bold mission: to improve safety, compliance, and convenience in CNG fuel station operations by digitizing tank verification and tracking.

August–September 2024

Research & Planning

Market research began, including interviews with pump operators, understanding CNG testing regulations, and validating the problem-solution fit.

October–November 2024

Prototype Development

Work began on building the MVP. Initial features included CNG tank test tracking, station registration, and a basic admin dashboard.

December 2024

Internal Testing

The prototype was tested internally with simulated station data to validate core functionalities like tank alerting and compliance logging.

January–February 2025

First Pilot Outreach

Approached initial CNG pump owners for free trials and pilot deployment. Early testing environments prepared for field feedback.

March 2025

Beta Testing Begins

First real-world pilot with selected CNG stations initiated. Feedback focused on usability, data accuracy, and interface improvements.
