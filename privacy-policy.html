Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as your name, email address, phone number, company name, and any other information you submit through our forms or services. We also collect data about your use of FuelLens, including device information, log data, and cookies.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to provide and improve our services, process registrations, communicate with you, send important updates, and ensure compliance with legal and regulatory requirements. We may also use your data for analytics and to personalize your experience.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You may also object to or restrict certain processing of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at info.fuellens@gmail.com.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or your data, contact us at info.fuellens@gmail.com.
