About FuelLens

FuelLens is dedicated to revolutionizing the CNG tank verification process through innovative technology and comprehensive compliance solutions. Our platform empowers CNG station owners and compliance officers to ensure safety, streamline operations, and stay ahead of regulatory requirements. With a focus on accuracy, efficiency, and user experience, FuelLens is your trusted partner in CNG management.

How FuelLens Works

1. Scan the Tank QR Code

Technicians or station staff scan the unique QR code on each CNG tank using the FuelLens app or dashboard.

2. Instant Compliance Check

FuelLens instantly checks the tank's compliance status and history in a secure, centralized database.

3. Automated Alerts & Reports

Receive real-time notifications for upcoming inspections, renewals, and compliance deadlines.

4. Analytics & Insights

Access detailed analytics and reports to optimize operations and ensure regulatory compliance.
